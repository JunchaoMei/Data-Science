4. Write down your observation based on these plots.¶

Centrality measures the importance of nodes in a network.

(1) "Full Monty, The (1997)" has the most degrees connected to it according to the Degree Centrality measure. If you think the number of linked movie nodes is most important, "Full Monty, The (1997)" is the most influential movie in the community.

(2) "As Good As It Gets (1997)", which has the highest Betweenness Centrality, can be though of a movie to bridge between different movie groups.

(3) "As Good As It Gets (1997)" has also the most Closeness Centrality. That can be interpreted that it is closest to all the other movie nodes.

(4) "Star Wars (1977)" has the highest PageRank. PageRank is a technique for estimating page quality. Considering the numbers of ratings of the movie nodes that direct to each movie, "Star Wars (1977)" is considered the most important movie.
